🔥 The FIRE Framework

The FIRE Framework is a lightweight, practical approach to surfacing and solving problems in rural healthcare settings. Designed for teams with limited time and resources, FIRE empowers frontline staff to take initiative and improve systems that affect their work every day.

FIRE stands for:

  • Find – Spot the issue or opportunity during your normal workday.
  • Identify – Get specific: What’s the root problem? Who’s affected? When and where?
  • Resolve – Try a fix or recommend a solution using what you have available.
  • Elevate – If it worked, share it. If it needs help, raise it to someone who can assist.

🛠️ Free Tools to Get Started

You don’t need fancy software or big budgets to launch FIRE. Here’s how to start today with free tools:

  • Google Form – Use this to collect frontline ideas and issues in real time.
  • Google Sheet – Track submissions, status, notes, and who’s working on what.
  • Email – Keep your team in the loop with updates or to ask for help.

Anyone can start a FIRE. One issue, one fix, one conversation at a time.
