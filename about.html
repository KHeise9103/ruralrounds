About RuralRounds

RuralRounds was built by two sisters — one works as a rural nurse in Minnesota, the other as a critical care provider who lives in spreadsheets and ICU alarms. We’ve both seen just how hard rural healthcare can be. It’s gritty, isolated, and full of moments where you think, “Why does no one talk about this stuff?”

This site is our answer to that question. A place to be real, to connect, and to share tools that actually make sense in the real world — the world where you might be the only one on call, where backup is two hours away, and where you're making do with what you have.

We're not here to sell you anything or make things sound prettier than they are. We're here to make things better — one story, one resource, one connection at a time.

What you'll find here:

  • Stories — the kind you won't find in journals
  • Tools — built with low-resources in mind
  • Community — or at least the start of one

If you're working in rural healthcare, this space is yours. Pull up a chair. Drop us a story. Ask a hard question. Or just poke around and know you're not alone out there.

We’re glad you’re here. And we’re just getting started.
